Why IRBs Work

"Preach the word; be prepared in season and out of season; correct, rebuke and encourage — with great patience and careful instruction."

— 2 Timothy 4:2
01

Real-Time Interaction

The IRB equips preachers, leaders, trainers, and conference presenters with a powerful way to interact in real time with groups, individuals, and crowds. The first group or person to press the button appears instantly on screen. Engagement increases immediately, attention sharpens, and time seems to fly when using this gospel tool.

02

Plug & Play Simplicity

We have purposefully engineered this Gospel tool with the end user in mind, whether you are highly tech savvy or have very little technical experience. Setup takes only moments. Connect your buttons, launch the interface, and begin interacting immediately. There are no complicated configurations, no steep learning curves, and no unnecessary distractions. Instead, you are free to focus on what truly matters: communicating truth clearly, engaging people meaningfully, and leading with confidence.

03

Flexibility & Scalability

From a room of hundreds gathered in groups to a small, elite leadership training setting, the IRBs scale effortlessly to fit your need. The uses are limitless – from powerful interactive teaching moments to playful conference icebreakers. The IRB system delivers large-scale impact while maintaining a personal, individual feel.

04

Control at Your Fingertips

Take full command of the entire IRB system from wherever you are using your phone, tablet, laptop, or even your smartwatch. Reset buzzers instantly and create, edit, or delete questions in seconds. Whether you are adjusting during a session or preparing beforehand, everything is designed to be intuitive, fast, and fully in your hands. Seamless control means fewer distractions and greater focus on meaningful interaction.
