Real-Time Interaction

The IRB equips preachers, leaders, trainers, and conference presenters with a powerful way to interact in real time with groups, individuals, and crowds. The first group or person to press the button appears instantly on screen. Engagement increases immediately, attention sharpens, and time seems to fly when using this gospel tool. What was once passive listening becomes active participation, creating energy, focus, and unforgettable moments.

Plug & Play Simplicity

We have purposefully engineered this Gospel tool with the end user in mind, whether you are highly tech-savvy or a complete novice leader. Setup takes moments. Connect your buttons, launch the interface, and begin interacting instantly. You only need to know a few simple basics to get started. From there, you can control the system from the back desk, front laptop, tablet, phone, or even a smartwatch. Watch this 5-minute video to see just how simple it really is.

Personal Teaching at Any Scale

From a room of hundreds gathered in groups to a small, elite leadership training setting, the IRBs scale effortlessly to fit your need. The uses are limitless — from powerful interactive teaching moments to playful conference icebreakers. The IRB system delivers large-scale impact while still maintaining a personal, individual feel for every participant.
