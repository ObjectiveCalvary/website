Game Rules

Discover how the Bible fits together as one unified story through engaging, competitive gameplay rooted in sound theology and trusted scholarship.

Getting Started

How to Win: Be the first player to get rid of all your cards!

  • 1
    Shuffle all cards together.
  • 2
    Deal 7 cards to each player.
  • 3
    Reveal 5 cards face-up — this is the Discovery Timeline.
  • 4
    Place the remaining cards face-down this is the draw pile.
💡 Tip — Want a simpler game? Use Old Testament or New Testament cards only. For an advanced challenge, combine and shuffle both decks together.

Taking Your Turn

On your turn, choose one of three actions: Draw, Place, or Ask.

  • 1
    Draw — Take a card from the bottom of a draw pile without looking. Or swap a card from the Discovery Timeline: place a card that fits between two adjacent cards, then take one of those adjacent cards.
  • 2
    Place — Lay cards face-up on the table if you hold any of the following:
    • 5 cards of the same theme/colour.
    • 3 cards in unbroken chronological order.
    • 1+ cards that extend an existing theme set already on the table.
  • 3
    Ask — Instead of drawing, ask a specific player: "Do you have a [theme/colour] card?" They must hand over all matching cards. If they have none, you must draw 2 cards.
⚔️ Challenge Rule — Think a card was placed incorrectly? Call "Challenge!" The group checks the placement together.

If the placement was wrong: That player takes the card back and draws 2 penalty cards.
If the challenger was wrong: The challenger draws 2 cards.
