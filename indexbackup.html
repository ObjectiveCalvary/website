Our Story

Why we built this

Objective Calvary was born from a simple but urgent conviction: that the crucifixion of Jesus Christ is the most theologically rich event in history, and yet one of the most poorly communicated. The Church has the message — but often lacks the gospel tools to make it land with the depth it deserves.

William and Dylan met at Stellenbosch, where a commerce degree and a masters in computer science collided with a shared faith and a restlessness to do something about it. Objective Calvary is the result — a ministry at the intersection of theology and technology, building discipleship resources and gospel tools that serve the local church without getting in the way of the Gospel.
